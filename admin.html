Watch YouTube Videos in Foreground on iOS Devices

iOS 9, available for iPad and iPhone, has an interest Picture In Picture (PIP) mode that lets you watch HTM5 videos in the foreground while multi-tasking - you can check your emails, read iBooks or explore Maps while the video is play in little window on top of all apps.

See: Always on Top for Windows

iOS 9 PIP mode is available for all HTML5 videos that you watch inside Safari but if you are using the official YouTube PIP, you cannot detach the video and bring it to the foreground. The YouTube app has to be active for the video to play in the foreground.

Enter YouTube PIP, a web app that lets you watch YouTube videos in PIP on your iPhone and iPad. Enter the YouTube video URL and play the embedded video. You It will embed the HTML5 version of the video that can be pulled out of Safari so you can continue multi-tasking.
